Why do people follow a brand on Instagram? E-commerce survey 2020

Many brands get this wrong.

Putting out ads & promotions in Instagram is a dead strategy. Many brand managers are not ready to change their perception of advertising when it comes to Social Media.

The only way to retain and to grow your Instagram audience is by becoming a publisher in your industry. ie, by creating entertaining/educational content which provides value for the audiences.

In order to clarify this statement, we did a survey regarding why Indian consumers choose to follow a brand on Instagram.
